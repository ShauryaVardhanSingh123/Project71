  • Grandparents Day celebrated in GD Goenka School

    As a tribute to grandparents, a special assembly on Grandparents’ Day was conducted by the students of GD Goenka School, Greater Noida. Children took a pledge that they will always give due regard and respect to their grandparents. “Grandparents are the foundation of our lives and are the roots of our families. Their importance was showcased through an amazing skit and a wonderful dance performance by the kids,” said Dr Renu Sehgal, Principal, GD Goenka School, Greater Noida.

  • Coronavirus shadow on toppers’ future plans

    Even after coming out with flying colours in CBSE Board class 12 examinations, two minds about their future plans in view of coronavirus pandemic. Many students wanted to move to other cities for higher education but the virus outbreak has forced them to revisit their plans. Some of them are ready to take the risk while others have started considering options available in the city.
